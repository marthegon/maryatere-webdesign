NewJeans is a South Korean girl group formed by ADOR.
The group is composed of five members: Minji, Hanni, Danielle, Haerin, and Hyein.
They are known for their girl next door image and 1990s-
and 2000s-indebted pop and R&B songs with influences of various dance and club styles.
